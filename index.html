RULES OF THE GAME

  • Choose a profile picture, then number of players and points;
  • click on the "open" button to play: you've to have at least 10 fiches to play
  • you've to click on "open" until some of the players have score (a pair, two pair ecc);
  • when the game is opened you've 7 seconds to change your cards, clicking on them;
  • select from the selector the fiches you want to play and press bet;
  • here starts the second round of the game:
    1. if the other players bet more than you, you can decide to stay (bet the same) or to leave and to bet;
    2. if you bet more than the others or you bet 0 in the first move, you can simply click on btn show;
  • Only the player who match the max bet on the table can partecipate the game and win;

    • DECIDE THE WINNER

    • the player who has the highest score wins;
    • if the scores among the players who match the max bet on table are the same then, the winner is who has the highest sum of the cards;
    • if the sum of the cards is the same among players, then the winner is choosen flipping a coin;
